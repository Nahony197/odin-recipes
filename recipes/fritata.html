Randy's goatcheese and heirloom tomato frittata

Description

Ingredients

  • Heirloom tomatoes
  • Shalots
  • Goatcheese
  • Butter
  • Olive oil
  • Crème fraîche

Steps

  1. Cut vegetables
  2. Slightly fry vegetable pieces in a pan
  3. Add some goatcheese
  4. Serve it with a little bit of crème fraîche on the top
  5. Finish the dish, go to sleep mode
